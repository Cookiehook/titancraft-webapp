Create New Location

Use this form to create a new location that is present on the Titancraft server. For example:

  • Your new shop selling enchanted books and tool repair services
  • A publicly accessible farm, or farm that you lease access to
  • Community locations such as the Entertainment District

When created, you will be added as the sole staff member, and redirected to the new page for that location. From there you can add stock/service/mob details, or change who staffs that location.
